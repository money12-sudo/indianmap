A geographic survey · working dataset

Indian commercial and institutional geography in metropolitan Houston, 1972–2026

This is a survey of where Houston's Indian population built its businesses and institutions, and when. It plots twenty-eight establishments — grocers, restaurants, jewelers, temples, gurdwaras, and civic organizations — by date of founding and by location, drawn from published sources, institutional records, and the contemporary press.

Two patterns are visible in the plotted data. Institutional founding precedes commercial founding by more than a decade: the first gurdwara dates to 1972 and the India Culture Center to 1973, while the earliest firmly dated Indian business on the Hillcroft corridor is from 1984. And the establishments distribute by type rather than settling into a single district — groceries in the suburbs where families live, high-value and infrequent trade concentrated on Hillcroft, and fine dining east of both, in the inner loop.

The map documents this distribution. It does not explain it. The question it raises — what social and economic function these businesses perform for a population dispersed across a metropolitan area without a dense residential enclave, and how that function varies by type — is the subject of ongoing work.

Founding date against distance from the corridor

Each establishment is plotted by year of founding and by distance from the intersection of Hillcroft Avenue and Harwin Drive. Two axes are available. The default measures east–west displacement; the alternate measures straight-line distance in any direction. The distinction matters for interpretation: an east–west axis registers only east–west variation, and will place an establishment eleven miles due north at the origin. The radial measure is the more accurate; the directional measure is retained because the received account of this community is specifically one of westward movement.

Evidence

A working dataset of establishments. Each record carries an evidence level; those marked research lead are unverified. Coordinates given as approximate or unverified have not been confirmed in the field. Where published sources disagree on a date, both are recorded and the disagreement noted. A full bibliography and a record-by-record evidence table appear on the sources page; the scholarly literature this work draws on is on the scholarship page.
